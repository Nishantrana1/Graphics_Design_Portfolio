THE ARTIST'S DOSSIER

About the Artist

Nishant is a graphic designer and video editor with an unwavering passion for vintage aesthetics, Art Deco grandeur, and the timeless craft of visual storytelling. With seasoned experience spanning logo design, brand identity, social media campaigns, print design, and cinematic video editing, he transforms every project into a curated exhibition of artistry. He thrives at the intersection of old-world craftsmanship and modern design innovation — blending the elegance of a bygone era with the precision of today's digital tools to create work that resonates, captivates, and endures.
